# Privacy Policy

**Last updated:** March 27, 2026

## Overview
This Privacy Policy describes how your information is handled in this application (the "App"). Your privacy is important, and the App is designed to collect and store as little information as possible.

## Information Collection and Use
The App does **not collect, transmit, or store any personal data externally**.

All data you enter into the App — including but not limited to instrument details, configurations, notes, and images — is stored **locally on your device only**.

## Local Data Storage
- All App data is stored in local JSON files on your device.
- No data is sent to any external servers or cloud services.
- No user accounts or sign-ins are required.

## Photo Library Access
The App may request access to your device’s photo library for the sole purpose of allowing you to:
- Import images into the App

Key points:
- Access is **read-only** and initiated by you
- The App does **not scan or access your photo library without your action**
- Imported images are stored locally within the App
- No images are uploaded, shared, or transmitted externally

## Data Sharing
The App does **not share any data** with third parties.

## Data Security
Because all data is stored locally on your device:
- You are responsible for maintaining the security of your device
- Deleting the App may result in permanent loss of all stored data

## Children’s Privacy
The App does not knowingly collect any personal information from children or users of any age.

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Any changes will be reflected by updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this document.
